# Terms of Service — otnaP

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Usage
otnaP is provided as-is as an orchestrator and routing bot for the *red Discord bot family. It is intended for personal and community use.

## Data
- otnaP does not store any user data or messages
- Queries sent to `/ask` are forwarded to the OpenRouter API (Gemini model) for processing — see [OpenRouter's Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy)
- No query content is stored permanently by otnaP

## Conduct
- Do not use otnaP to abuse or spam the AI routing feature
- The bot owner reserves the right to shut down the bot or revoke access at any time

## Third-party services
When `/ask` is used, queries are sent to OpenRouter's API. By using this feature you acknowledge that your input is processed by a third-party AI provider.

## Liability
The developers of otnaP are not responsible for incorrect routing suggestions, AI model responses, or downtime of any connected *red bot.
