# Privacy Policy — Pred

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## What we collect
- Discord user IDs (to identify poll authors)
- Guild IDs and channel IDs (to track where polls were created)
- Poll questions and options (submitted by users)

## What we do NOT collect
- Usernames, avatars, email addresses
- Message content outside of slash command interactions
- Any data beyond what is required for poll functionality

## Data storage
All data is stored locally in `polls.json` on the server running the bot. It is not transmitted to any external service.

## Data deletion
To remove your data, contact the server owner or bot operator.
