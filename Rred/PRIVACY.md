# Privacy Policy — Rred

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## What we collect
- Discord user IDs (to deliver reminders)
- Channel IDs (to send reminder notifications)
- Reminder text and scheduled time (submitted by users)

## What we do NOT collect
- Usernames, avatars, email addresses
- Message content outside of slash command interactions

## Data storage
All data is stored locally in `reminders.json`. It is not transmitted to any external service.

## Data deletion
Reminders are automatically deleted after they are delivered. To remove pending reminders, use `/cancel` or contact the bot operator.
