# Terms of Service — Rred

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Usage
Rred is provided as-is for personal and community use on Discord servers.

## Data
- Reminder content and delivery times are stored locally in `reminders.json`
- Discord user IDs are stored solely to deliver reminders to the correct user
- Data is not shared with any third parties

## Conduct
- Do not use Rred to spam channels or users
- The bot owner reserves the right to cancel any reminder or shut down the bot at any time

## Liability
The developers of Rred are not responsible for missed reminders due to bot downtime or network issues.
