# Privacy Policy — Ared

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## What we collect
- Discord user IDs (for warning records)
- Guild IDs (to scope warnings per server)
- Warning reasons and moderator names (submitted by server moderators)

## What we do NOT collect
- Usernames, avatars, email addresses
- Message content outside of slash command interactions

## Data storage
All data is stored locally in `warnings.json`. It is not transmitted to any external service.

## Data deletion
To remove warning records for a user, use `/clearwarns`. To remove all data, delete `warnings.json` or contact the bot operator.
