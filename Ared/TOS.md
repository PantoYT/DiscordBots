# Terms of Service — Ared

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Usage
Ared is provided as-is for moderation use on Discord servers.

## Data
- Warning records (user ID, reason, moderator, date) are stored locally in `warnings.json`
- Data is not shared with any third parties

## Conduct
- Ared must only be used in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
- Do not use Ared to harass, discriminate against, or unfairly punish users
- The bot owner reserves the right to shut down the bot at any time

## Liability
The developers of Ared are not responsible for moderation decisions made using this bot. Server administrators are solely responsible for how moderation powers are exercised.
